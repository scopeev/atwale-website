Registered non-profit making Christian organization

Atawale Child Support Uganda is a registered non-profit making Christian organization with a purpose of supporting the orphaned and vulnerable children.
Supporting our children to attain basic education and access basic necessities.
Helping the orphaned and vulnerable children with a purpose of demonstrating the love of God and nurturing them into God fearing citizens.
